# 📊 E-commerce Sales Analysis (Superstore Dataset) with python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Superstore E-commerce dataset to understand sales performance, profitability, and customer purchasing patterns. The objective is to identify sales trends, analyze product performance, and evaluate customer segments using Python.

The analysis was conducted using **Pandas for data manipulation** and **Plotly for interactive visualizations**.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the **Superstore dataset**, which contains detailed transactional information for an e-commerce business.

### Key Columns

* Order Date
* Ship Date
* Category
* Sub-Category
* Sales
* Profit
* Segment

These columns help analyze product performance, profitability, and customer segmentation.

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis

### 📅 Monthly Sales Analysis

Analyzed monthly sales to identify sales trends and seasonal patterns.

### 🛍️ Sales by Category

Compared total sales across major product categories:

* Furniture
* Technology
* Office Supplies

### 📦 Sales by Sub-Category

Identified which sub-categories generate the highest revenue.

### 💰 Monthly Profit Analysis

Evaluated monthly profit trends to understand overall business profitability.

### 📊 Profit by Category and Sub-Category

Analyzed profit contribution across different product categories and sub-categories.

### 👥 Sales and Profit by Customer Segment

Compared sales and profit across customer segments:

* Consumer
* Corporate
* Home Office

### 📉 Sales to Profit Ratio Analysis

Calculated the **Sales to Profit Ratio** to evaluate product profitability.

Formula used:

Sales to Profit Ratio = Sales / Profit

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Some sub-categories generate **high sales but relatively low profit**.
* The **Consumer segment contributes the highest overall sales**.
* Sales and profit show **variation across different months**.
* Certain product categories perform **better in terms of profitability**.
